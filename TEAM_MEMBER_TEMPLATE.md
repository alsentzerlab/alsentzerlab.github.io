# Team Member Template

## Instructions:
1. Upload the person's photo to `/assets/img/` folder
2. Copy the appropriate template below
3. Replace ALL_CAPS placeholders with actual information
4. Paste into the correct section in `_pages/team.md`

---

## TEMPLATE FOR REGULAR TEAM MEMBER (with website):
```html

  <a href="WEBSITE_URL_HERE" target="_blank">
```
